Decisioninja is a minimalist random decision maker app with Material You design.

Features:

- Dice roller (1-6 dice, custom die sizes)
- Coin flip with animated flip
- Random pointer wheel for choices
- Binary choice picker (Yes/No, Heads/Tails, etc.)
- History of recent decisions
- Beautiful Material You theming (dynamic colors)
- Dark and light mode support
- Fully localized (English, Italian, French, German, Spanish, Japanese)
- No ads, no tracking, fully open source
